  # Privacy Policy

  **Last Updated: April 17, 2026**

  ## 1. Introduction
  This Privacy Policy explains how songane-agency ("the App"), operated by Songane Agency, collects,
  uses, and protects information when integrating with TikTok and other platform APIs.

  ## 2. Information We Collect
  - **Account Information**: When the channel owner authorizes the App, we receive the platform user
  ID and an access token necessary to upload and manage content.
  - **Content Metadata**: Titles, descriptions, tags, and analytics of videos uploaded through the
  App.

  We do not collect personal information from end users who view content on platforms such as TikTok.

  ## 3. How We Use Information
  - To upload, schedule, and manage videos on authorized accounts.
  - To retrieve performance metrics for internal reporting.

  ## 4. Information Sharing
  We do not sell, rent, or share account data with third parties. Data is used solely for the
  operation of the App.

  ## 5. Data Retention
  Access tokens are stored securely and are revoked upon account disconnection. Content metadata is
  retained for internal analytics unless deletion is requested.

  ## 6. Security
  We use reasonable technical measures to protect data, including encrypted storage of access tokens.

  ## 7. Channel Owner Rights
  The channel owner may revoke the App's access at any time via the platform's Security & Login
  settings (e.g., TikTok Settings → Security → Manage Apps).

  ## 8. Third-Party Services
  The App integrates with TikTok and may integrate with Meta (Facebook/Instagram/Threads), YouTube,
  and Naver APIs. Use of those services is governed by their respective privacy policies.

  ## 9. Changes
  We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use constitutes acceptance.
